Sectors

Payment Service Providers

Payment Service Providers enable business owners use payment methods like credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets both in-person and online transactions..

e-Money Institutions

E-Money Institutions provide a platform where payment transactions can be conducted via electronic money.

Cryptocurrencies & Virtual Financial Assets

VFA is a form of digital medium recordation which is used as a unit of account, store of value or a digital medium of exchange.

FinTech

Fintech aims to improve the use and delivery of financial services by the help of technology integration.

Blockchain

Blockchain refers to a digital ledger that is designed to facilitate the process of transactions across a network of computer systems

IoT and Artificial Intelligence

IoT refers to internet-connected objects that are embedded with technologies like softwares and sensors which is able to collect and transfer data. Artificial Intelligence is the branch of computer sciences that simulates human intelligence processes by machines.

Robotics

Robotics is a branch of engineering which manufactures, designs, operates and deals with physical robots.

e-Commerce

E-Commerce refers to the activity of buying and selling of goods and services over an electronic network.

Quick Commerce

Quick Commerce is a form of new and faster e-commerce.

Mobility

Mobility is the industry of transporting people and goods.

MedTech

Medical Technology includes every product, service or solution that aims to improve people’s health by using medical technology.

Life Sciences

Life Sciences refers to sciences that concentrates on living organisms including biology, zoology, biochemistry, botany, microbiology and other related subjects.

Research & Development

R&D includes certain type of activities the aim of which is to innovate and introduce new products and services.

Drones

Drones are any unpiloted aircrafts designed to carry out range of tasks, ranging from military operations to delivery services.

3D Printing

3D printing is a process of manufacturing three dimensional solid objects from a digital file.

Agriculture Technology

Agriculture Technology is the application of techniques for the purposes of growth control and harvesting animal and vegetable products.

Visual Arts

Visual arts refer to any form of art which was produced by using visual perception such as graphics, decorative arts, print making, photography and filmmaking.

ICT Shared Services

Information and Communications Technology is a term used for IT that integrates the use of telecommunications and computers as well as other types of software that enables users to access, store, manipulate information.

Video Game Development & e-Sports

Video game development industry aims to design, develop and release games based on a planned concept. E-Sports refers to a form of competitive, organised video gaming.

Film

Film industry comprises the technological and commercial institutions of filmmaking, such as production, screenwriting, cinematography, animation etc.
