About Us

Let's succeed together in Europe!


In the fast-paced world of startups, the landscape is constantly evolving, with technology swiftly advancing and competition intensifying. It's no longer a choice but a necessity for startups to digitalize and globalize to unlock their full potential and thrive. Transitioning from traditional operations to digital workflows has become the key to survival and success.

Recognizing the rising need for digital transformation, Startup In EU was established. Our goal is to empower startups to harness the top-notch services and diverse opportunities Europe, and particularly Malta, offers. We provide a comprehensive array of services and innovative solutions, paving the way for startups to successfully undertake their digital transformation journey.

Europe's diverse opportunities and robust ecosystem have positioned it as a magnet for globally-minded founders and tech companies eager to broaden their reach and build a resilient future for their startups. With Malta as our focus, we at Startup In EU bridge the gap between your innovative startup and the thriving European startup scene.

If you're ready to cross this digital bridge and expand your horizons in Malta, contact Startup In EU today. Together, we can chart the course for your startup's successful journey in Europe.

What is Start in EU?


At Startup In EU, our top priority is the success and growth of our clients. We are a dedicated service provider that aims to empower startups and help them thrive within the Maltese ecosystem.



By leveraging our expertise and comprehensive range of services, we enable startups to tap into the abundant benefits offered by the Maltese startup ecosystem. Our goal is to create an environment where startups can flourish and achieve their full potential.



Through strategic partnerships and collaborations with startups, incubators, accelerators, and both public and private sectors, we ensure that our clients have access to the best resources, support, and opportunities available. We work tirelessly to make the EU one of the most advantageous and rewarding destinations for startups.

With Startup In EU by your side, you can expect tailored guidance, innovative solutions, and a network of like-minded professionals who are committed to helping you succeed. Together, we will navigate the intricacies of the Maltese startup landscape and unlock a world of opportunities for your startup's growth and prosperity.

What exactly does Start in EU do?


Startup In EU is driven by the success and benefit of our clients. We are dedicated to strengthening the EU startup ecosystem to create a highly advantageous environment for startups to thrive.

Our efforts revolve around uniting and building a supportive community through various events and activities. By fostering collaboration and diversity in age, gender, culture, and backgrounds, we create a vibrant ecosystem that offers tremendous opportunities for startups.



We understand the importance of accessible resources and knowledge for startups. That's why we work diligently to ensure that our clients have access to valuable resources and vital know-how. Through education and support, we empower startups to overcome challenges and achieve their goals.

Moreover, we actively address regulatory issues and barriers that impede startup operations and growth within the EU. By advocating for startup-friendly regulations, we streamline processes and remove complexities, making it easier for startups to operate, invest, and raise funds.



At Startup In EU, our primary focus is on the benefit of our clients. We strive to provide tailored guidance, networking opportunities, and a supportive ecosystem that propels their success. With our expertise and resources, startups can leverage the EU startup ecosystem to unlock their full potential and realize their dreams.
